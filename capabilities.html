Enterprise Capabilities

Built to Build. Designed to Scale.

Four integrated service groups that move businesses from ideation to actualization — formulated, designed, built, launched and managed on behalf of global clients.

01

Entrepreneurship

The fast pace in the rise of global unemployment has become an albatross around the neck of governments all over the world. As a Leading Venture Management Architect Firm, the main focus in eradicating the societal menace of unemployment is through entrepreneurship.

Global Community of Purpose (GCOP) Zero Capital Business Services Multi Multi Millionaires (3M) Young Entrepreneurs Club

Global Community of Purpose (GCOP)

An Entrepreneurial Initiative that brings together diverse entrepreneur brains among the youth with the same purpose across diverse industries to create jobs through business ownership. Provides an ecosystem encompassing entrepreneurship leadership, business planning, financial modelling, marketing, legal framework and financial engineering.

Zero Capital Business Services

Start your own business with zero startup capital at Business District. The firm takes the stress out from starting a business by providing clients with assets such as fully furnished office space, office equipment, professional expertise and more to start their own business and gradually grow through the various business phases.

Multi Multi Millionaires (3M)

Business District provides children from seven (7) years old with the necessary entrepreneurial training skills that will groom them to be self-sufficient and independent global entrepreneurs. Parents and guardians can create a legacy for their children by establishing businesses managed efficiently on their behalf by professionals.

02

Venture Management Architect

Business District are stewards in custody of the businesses they build for clients — ensuring their survival and sustainability both in the short and long term through professionally trained and experienced Venture Management Architects. The firm specializes in the leadership of actualizing and commercializing the dreams of entrepreneurs, building and managing businesses on behalf of individual investors and corporate institutions both locally and internationally.

Succession Venture Architect PPP Venture Architect Family Custodial Venture Architect Distressed Venture Architect Incubation Venture Architect Growth Venture Architect Philanthropic Venture Architect Golden Operational Finance Growth & Venture Capital Management

Succession Venture Architect

Succession Planning Architects are trained to seamlessly step into a founder's shoes and manage their business on their behalf, ensuring the founder's vision is preserved — both for temporal emergencies and permanent succession.

PPP Venture Architect

Specialized PPP Venture Architects provide management services for government agencies, state-owned enterprises, public institutions and government majority interests in private companies — with a clear structure of separation of powers for impartial decision making.

Family Custodial & Judicial Venture Architect

Family Venture Architects provide experienced, neutral and impartial professionals to manage family businesses in the common interest of all members — including families in litigation for corporate inheritance, acting as independent custodians.

Distressed Venture Architect

Distressed Venture Architects design and assemble pro-active solutions to aid in the restructuring of distressed businesses, providing an overhaul corporate distress architecture for organizations experiencing seasonal, systematic and bankruptcy distress.

Incubation Venture Architect

Provides assistance to register a business, set up an office, recruit staff and handle start-up essentials — including business identification and formation, business modelling and strategy, financial modelling, recruitment and operational management.

Growth Venture Architect

Growth Venture Architects understand the industry dynamics that drive growth for every business, assisting companies to graduate their growth potential from one phase to another with sustainable, attainable and replicable growth models.

03

Economic & Management Consulting

Comprehensive consulting across Banking & Credit Risk, Insurance, Capital Markets and Public Sector — partnering with Multilateral Development Banks, DFIs, Commercial Banks, Non-Bank Financial Institutions, Microfinance, Insurance firms, Exchange Houses, Brokerage Firms, Asset Managers, Central Banks and State Owned Enterprises.

Credit Risk Architect Sovereign Debt Advisory Insurance Architecture Capital Markets Public Sector & SOEs Corporate Finance Advisory International Business

Credit Risk Architect

A modern and proactive risk management architecture mechanism offering solutions to mitigating credit default and recovery in the financial industry — from Sovereign Credit Risk (MDB debt negotiations) to 3rd System Credit Risk Management and Credit Forensic Architecture (CFA).

Insurance Architecture

In-depth knowledge in underwriting, premiums, claims, investments and more — building a strong corporate foundation for growth in the insurance business through operational optimization, financial engineering and third party management.

Capital Markets

Supporting Exchange Houses, Brokerage Firms, Asset & Wealth Managers, Private Equity and Venture Capital with management performance, market strategy, product design, portfolio management, term leadership and rating intelligence.

Public Sector & SOEs

Supporting central banks, governments and SOEs through financial policy architecture design, monetary policy control, sovereign debt advisory, fiscal policy planning, economic development planning and dedicated Venture Architect management expertise.

Corporate Finance Advisory

Strategic insight into corporate finance matters including valuation, mergers & acquisitions advisory, debt management advisory, cost-benefit analytics, tax advisory, financial engineering and audit assurance & inception reports.

International Business

The International Enterprise Clinic (IEC) provides a unique solution for international businesses and investors seeking to undertake business transactions and operations in Africa — leveraging fixed and variable assets to provide a reliable partner on the continent.

04

Agro Business & Technology Innovation

Agricultural innovation transforms farmers into agro business entrepreneurs and projects agriculture as a lucrative business enterprise through technology innovation and digitization using artificial intelligence to create a major source of employment in Africa. New agricultural equipment is used to enhance agricultural businesses desiring to expand their operations by injecting innovative and dynamic ideas through strategic partnerships.

Farm Management Assembling Plant Management Value Addition Management Storage & Warehouse Management

Farm Management

Expertise in broad-base farm management from cultivation to harvesting across Crop, Animal and Aquaculture domains. Cutting-edge farming business models and digitization of the farming process are employed for excellent and quality management delivery, with Global and Regional Food Security as a main goal.

Value Addition Management

Promotes value addition to agricultural produce through technology innovation, research and scientific systems to enhance agriculture as a profitable venture. Product design and packaging are done with international market standards as the benchmark with the intention to be competitive on the global stage.

Storage & Warehouse Management

Specialized Venture Architects efficiently manage storage and warehousing facilities, leveraging Digital Transition to provide exceptional management. Ensures produce is maintained at appropriate temperatures in good condition, with guaranteed safety and security of managed warehouses.
